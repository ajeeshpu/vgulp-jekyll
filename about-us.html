About Vgulp

Vgulp helps drinkers like you find awesome bars and liquor stores, and save some dough through claiming the deals and discounts we offer.

Why drinkers love Vgulp

  • Simply put, more options – Instead of giving you discounts in places you never want to go to, which are 10 – 15 kms from your work / home, we negotiate deals in your neighborhood and provide you more options. When you get access to Vgulp, probably you will be surprised to find the same bars / pubs / liquor stores that you frequent welcome you with a deal. We have more deals in bars today, as compared to any other portal and the future is bright.

  • We don’t spam you with Daily Deals – We personally opted out of the various daily deals newsletters. Currently there is just one newsletter on Friday – “F for Freakin Awesome Fridays”, and occasional updates about how we are going forward.

  • No Crap Strategy – We will not put a single deal on Vgulp, that we personally wouldn’t claim. Vgulp was born out of a requirement to solve a pain we had, and we will continue to do so.

  • No payments on the site – We believe payments cannot out perform convenience. We would not charge you a dime for claiming a deal on our site, neither would we ask you to print coupons.
